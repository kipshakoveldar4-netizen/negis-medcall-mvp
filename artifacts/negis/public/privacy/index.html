Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2026

Negis is a CRM and automation platform for clinics. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when clinics and users use our service.

Information We Collect

We may collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, clinic names, employee information, customer and lead information, appointment data, advertising source data, and technical data required to operate the service.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the CRM, process leads, manage appointments, manage employees and branches, provide analytics, support users, improve the service, and enable integrations requested by the user.

Meta and Advertising Integrations

If a clinic connects Facebook, Instagram, or other advertising integrations, Negis may process advertising account data, campaign statistics, lead data, and related information. This data is used only to provide CRM and analytics functionality for the connected clinic account.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers required to operate Negis, such as hosting, database, notification, analytics, and integration providers.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their data by following the instructions on our Data Deletion page: https://www.negis.online/data-deletion/
