Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 17, 2026

If you want to request deletion of your data from Negis, please email us at: agentconcept01@gmail.com

What to Include in Your Request

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address or phone number
  • Your clinic name, if applicable
  • A clear statement that you want your data deleted from Negis
  • If your data came from Facebook or Instagram Lead Ads, please mention that in your request

Processing Time

We will review and process deletion requests within 30 days, unless we are required to retain certain data by law or contractual obligations.

Meta / Facebook Data

If your data was received through Facebook or Instagram integrations, we will delete the related lead or advertising data stored in Negis for the connected clinic account.
